## Context

Hard absorbing boundaries (barrier options, American-style optimal stopping, killed diffusions) require an indicator projector `𝟙_R` (multiply by zero in the killed region). The existing `KillingChernoff` (v2.6.0, ADR-0068) uses an exponential Butko-2018 soft-killing factor `e^{−κ·τ·𝟙_R}` → hard wall in the limit κ→∞; `Killing2ndChernoff` (v7.0.0, ADR-0126) achieves order-2 for soft-killing. Contradiction C3 (declared IRREDUCIBLE for the indicator): the indicator `𝟙_R` is idempotent (`𝟙_R² = 𝟙_R`), so the symmetric Strang composition `√K · flow · √K` collapses to over-killing (two half-steps of an idempotent operator), and the error estimate for the Strang splitting degenerates to order-1 with a non-removable O(τ) term. The ВПР: the shipped resolvent `(I + ε κ 𝟙_R)⁻¹` with `ε ∝ dx` is a smoothed approximation of the sharp projector that is non-idempotent at finite `ε`, relaxing the Strang collapse.

## Decision

Design `HardWallOrder2Chernoff<C, R, F>`: a resolvent-smoothed projector `P_ε = (I + ε κ 𝟙_R)⁻¹` with `ε = α·dx` acting as a TRIZ-3 (local-quality) boundary-zone intermediary — only the cells adjacent to the wall see the smoothed projector; interior cells see the exact kill. The symmetric Strang `P_ε^{1/2} · C(τ) · P_ε^{1/2}` then has a non-degenerate BCH residual and recovers order-2 in the operative zone (wall layer), matching the interior rate in the L²-norm. TRIZ-9 (prior anti-action — regularize before the symmetrization so it arrives at the composition step already tamed) + TRIZ-24 (intermediary — `P_ε` mediates between the exact kill and the flow) + TRIZ-3 (local quality — smoothing applied only at the boundary, not globally). Gate `G_HARD_WALL_ORDER2`: self-convergence of a hard-barrier problem with `ε = α·dx`, slope ≤ −1.95. Prerequisite: Barles–Souganidis-compatible convergence proof for the `ε→0` limit must be drafted in math.md before the gate is declared blocking.

## Consequences

Order-2 convergence for ALL hard-barrier / American / optimal-stopping problems in the library (moat: competitors either stay order-1 with hard walls or require explicit linear solves per time step). Extends naturally to `ObstacleChernoff` variational inequalities (ADR-0116) and to the `ReflectedHeatChernoff` image-method (v2.8.0, ADR-0072). Risk is MEDIUM: the `ε→0` limit convergence argument requires care to avoid viscosity-solution consistency breakdown; the convergence proof is semi-research and may surface a further amendment before the gate is declared.
